Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

OntoExhibit is an innovative ontology that accurately and extensively models museums, exhibitions, and artworks. Designed to capture the complexity and richness of these cultural elements, OntoExhibit provides a semantic framework that enables the organization and coherent linking of information.
